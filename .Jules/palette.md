## 2024-05-15 - Interactive Focus Indicators
**Learning:** This application's interactive elements (links, cards, and buttons) lack visible focus indicators and explicit hover states, leading to poor keyboard accessibility and an unresponsive feel.
**Action:** Consistently apply `outline-none focus:ring focus:ring-sky-600` alongside hover classes and `transition` modifiers to all interactive elements to ensure they are accessible and provide clear visual feedback.
